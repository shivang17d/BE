Story

Once there was a speedy rabbit he challenged a slow tortoise to a race, always boasting about his speed.

The race began, and the rabbit was running ahead while the tortoise was far behind.

Confident of victory, the rabbit ate a cabbage.

after a meal, the rabbit was sleeping under the tree.

When rabbit awake he saw the tortoise near the finish line.

The tortoise won the race, teaching the rabbit a valuable lesson: to not mock others and to respect their pace.
